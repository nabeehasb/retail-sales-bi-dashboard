# Retail Sales Analytics & Business Intelligence Dashboard

## Overview
An end-to-end retail analytics pipeline that processes sales data using Apache Spark, 
stores and queries it in Google BigQuery, and visualizes key business metrics through 
an interactive Power BI dashboard.

## Pipeline & Tools
- **Apache Spark** — data cleaning and large-scale transformation
- **Google BigQuery** — data storage and SQL-based aggregation
- **Power BI** — interactive dashboard and KPI visualization

## KPIs Tracked
- Total sales / revenue
- Customer trends
- Invoice volume
- Product performance

## Key Findings
-United Kingdom generated the highest revenue among all countries in the dataset
-The dashboard tracks KPIs: Total Sales, Total Customers, Total Invoices, and Total Quantity Sold
-Dashboard visualizations include: Sales by Country, Monthly Revenue Trends, Top Products by Sales,  Revenue Distribution by Country, and geographic sales mapping
-Analysis identified specific products with consistently high sales volume (top-selling products by  quantity)
-Monthly sales trends varied, with certain months showing notably higher order/sales activity than   others
-Average customer revenue was compared across countries to identify regions with stronger purchasing power, and highest-spending customers were identified individually
